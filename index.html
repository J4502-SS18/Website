Tateanna McCaskill

Tateanna is a Chicago native majoring in data journalism at the University of Missouri. She currently works as a digital news editor for the Columbia Missourian. Last year, she created content for the online publication PolitiFact and before that, she worked as a graphic designer. She has a deep interest in the innovation of media and programming. Now she collaborates with a team of Multimedia Planning and Design students to create a desktop and mobile website for Push, an open-source mobile news app. Reynolds Journalism Institute fellow Christopher Guess is the owner of the app.
